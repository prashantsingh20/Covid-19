Symptoms of Coronavirus

COVID-19 affects different People in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

High Fever

In early 2020, a new virus started making headlines for causing the disease known as COVID-19. One of the telltale symptoms of COVID-19 is a low-grade fever that gradually worsens over time.

Cough

You should see a doctor if you cough up blood or have a "barking" cough. You should also contact them if your cough hasn't improved with a few weeks, as this could indicate something more serious.

Sore Troath

Most sore throats are caused by infections, or by environmental factors like dry air Although a sore throat can be uncomfortable,it'll usually go away on its own.

Headache

Primary causes of headaches are causes that aren't related to separate medical conditions.These headaches are the result of an underlying process in the brain.
